* Senior Machine Learning Engineer
* Senior Full Stack Architect
* Enterprise Banking Solution Architect
* Senior UX Researcher
* Senior Product Designer
* Senior UI Designer
* AI Governance Specialist
* Responsible AI Expert

Your task is to design a **world-class enterprise Human-Centered Explainable AI platform for Intelligent Loan Approval**.
